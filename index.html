What we do

We are working to change how roads are made to prevent death and accidents because of bad road conditions.

Induction machine

Technology that creates heat to increase the temperature of to accelerate healing of asphalt.

Steel Fiber Asphalt mixtures

Our steel fibers mixtures conduct heat more effectively than conventional asphalt mixtures to enhance the self-healing properties of asphalt.
